Design System Guide

Core principles and constraints for designing on the ACE platform.

Core Constraints

  1. Glyphs over emoji. Always. ♠ ♥ ♦ ♣ ✦ ✕ ⇌ ◈ — never 🃏 🎰.
  2. Numbers are tabular-nums with $. $1,250, +$50, never 1.25k.
  3. Pronoun is "You". Never "the player".
  4. Action buttons: Title Case (Hit). Hero CTA: DEAL in caps.
  5. Felt radius is 40px+ with gold rim and inset shadow — that's the signature.
  6. Outcomes: Win = green glow, Loss = red, Push = amber, BJ = gold gradient.
  7. Chip denominations: $1 gray, $5 red, $10 blue, $25 amber, $50 green, $100 black/gold, $500 purple.

Voice & Tone

Confident, terse, casino-floor. Direct verbs. Never apologetic.

Place your bet to begin · Confirm Bet — $25 · Insurance costs $12 · pays 2:1 · Dealer shows Ace · New Round

Type Stack

  • Display: Outfit (700/800/900) — wordmarks, hero CTAs, balance, hand badges
  • Body: Inter (400/500/600) — everything else
  • Mono: JetBrains Mono (500/700) — eyebrows, labels, dollar amounts, hand values

Color Philosophy

Deep night sky over green felt, lit by amber gold. Backgrounds are nearly-black navy (#050A0F) with a soft radial wash. The felt is classic casino green. All accents are warm gold. Outcome colors are saturated green/red, not pastel. Imagery skews cool with warm gold highlights — never warm overall.
